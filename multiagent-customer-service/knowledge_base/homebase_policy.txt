BRAND OVERVIEW
HomeBase Supply is RetailCore Group's home improvement and hardware brand,
serving contractors, DIY homeowners, and property managers across the United
States. We carry over 40,000 products across tools, appliances, lumber,
plumbing, electrical, and outdoor equipment.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES
- Power Tools and Hand Tools (DeWalt, Milwaukee, Makita, HomeBase brand)
- Major Appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ranges)
- Outdoor Equipment (lawn mowers, leaf blowers, pressure washers, generators)
- Plumbing and Electrical Supplies
- Building Materials and Lumber
- Paint and Finishing Supplies
- Smart Home Installation Products

RETURN AND EXCHANGE POLICY
- 90-day return window for most products (longest in the industry)
- Major appliances: 30-day return window from delivery date
- Power tools: 30-day return, must be in working condition
- Lumber, cut materials, and custom orders are non-returnable
- Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals) non-returnable once opened
- Refunds processed in 5-7 business days
- In-store credit option available immediately

MAJOR APPLIANCE DELIVERY AND INSTALLATION
- Free delivery on appliances over $399
- Professional installation available for $149 (washers, dryers, dishwashers)
- Old appliance haul-away: $25 per unit
- Delivery window: 2-hour arrival window, day before confirmation call
- Damaged on delivery: report within 48 hours for immediate replacement

WARRANTY AND PROTECTION PLANS
- Manufacturer warranty honored on all products
- HomeBase Protection Plan available: 2-year ($39), 3-year ($69), 5-year ($99)
- Protection plan covers parts, labor, and power surge damage
- No deductibles on protection plan claims
- Claim processing: 24-48 hours for parts, 3-5 days for technician dispatch

CONTRACTOR ACCOUNTS
- HomeBase Pro accounts available for licensed contractors
- 10% discount on all purchases
- Net-30 payment terms available with approved credit
- Dedicated Pro desk at all locations: Monday-Friday 6AM-7PM

TROUBLESHOOTING ESCALATION PATHS
Major Appliances:
- Step 1: Check power connection and circuit breaker
- Step 2: Consult product manual for error codes
- Step 3: Run self-diagnostic if available
- Step 4: Schedule technician visit through HomeBase Protection Plan

Power Tools:
- Step 1: Check battery charge and connections
- Step 2: Inspect for visible damage
- Step 3: Test with different battery if cordless
- Step 4: If under warranty, process exchange at nearest location

ESCALATION TRIGGERS
Escalate immediately to human agent when:
- Appliance delivery damage reported
- Gas appliance installation safety concern raised
- Injury reported related to product use
- Contractor account billing dispute over $1,000
- Customer reports structural damage from product failure
