RETAILCORE GROUP — GLOBAL CUSTOMER SERVICE POLICY
Parent Company Policy Document | Applies to All Subsidiaries
Version 2.1 | Effective January 1, 2024

COMPANY OVERVIEW
RetailCore Group is a diversified retail holding company operating three consumer-facing brands:
- NovaTech Electronics: Consumer electronics, smart home devices, routers, and mobile accessories
- HomeBase Supply: Home improvement, tools, appliances, and outdoor equipment
- StyleMax Retail: Fashion, apparel, footwear, and lifestyle accessories

GLOBAL RETURN POLICY
All RetailCore subsidiaries honor the following minimum return standards:
- 30-day return window from date of purchase for all products
- Original receipt or order confirmation required
- Items must be in original or gently used condition
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method
- Exchange available at any RetailCore subsidiary location

ESCALATION STANDARDS
All subsidiary agents must escalate to a human representative when:
- Customer expresses frustration three or more times in a single interaction
- Issue involves a purchase over $500
- Customer explicitly requests a human agent
- Issue cannot be resolved within two agent exchanges
- Legal, safety, or injury concerns are raised

HUMAN HANDOFF PROTOCOL
When escalating, the AI agent must:
1. Acknowledge the customer's frustration directly
2. Confirm a human representative will follow up
3. Provide estimated wait time (standard: under 10 minutes during business hours)
4. Generate a case summary for the human agent
5. Never leave the customer without a clear next step

BUSINESS HOURS
Monday through Friday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern
Saturday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern
Sunday: 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern
After hours: Case logged, human callback within 2 business hours next day

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION COMMITMENT
RetailCore Group targets a Net Promoter Score of 70 or above across all brands.
Agents are instructed to prioritize genuine resolution over policy deflection.
If policy prevents resolution, escalate immediately — do not loop the customer.
