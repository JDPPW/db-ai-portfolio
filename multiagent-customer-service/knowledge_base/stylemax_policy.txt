STYLEMAX RETAIL — CUSTOMER SERVICE POLICY
A RetailCore Group Company
Version 1.9 | Effective January 1, 2024

BRAND OVERVIEW
StyleMax Retail is RetailCore Group's fashion and lifestyle brand, offering
contemporary apparel, footwear, accessories, and lifestyle products for men,
women, and children. We operate 280 retail locations and a full e-commerce
platform serving customers nationwide.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES
- Women's Apparel (casual, professional, activewear, outerwear)
- Men's Apparel (casual, professional, activewear, outerwear)
- Children's Clothing (newborn through teen)
- Footwear (all genders and ages)
- Accessories (bags, belts, jewelry, sunglasses, hats)
- Lifestyle and Home Decor (select locations)

RETURN AND EXCHANGE POLICY
- 60-day return window from purchase date
- Items must have original tags attached
- Worn, washed, or altered items are non-returnable
- Final sale items (marked with red tag) are non-returnable
- Swimwear and intimate apparel non-returnable for hygiene reasons
- Online returns: free return shipping label provided
- In-store returns: immediate refund or exchange processed
- Refunds to original payment method in 3-5 business days
- StyleMax gift cards available as immediate exchange option

STYLEMAX REWARDS PROGRAM
- Free to join, earn 1 point per $1 spent
- 500 points = $5 reward certificate
- Gold status at $500 annual spend: 1.5x points, free shipping always
- Platinum status at $1,000 annual spend: 2x points, early access to sales
- Birthday bonus: 2x points during birthday month
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity

SIZING AND FIT POLICY
- Free exchanges for sizing issues within 60 days
- Size exchange processed immediately in store
- Online size exchange: prepaid return label plus priority reshipment
- StyleMax FitGuide available on website and app for all products

DAMAGE AND DEFECT POLICY
- Manufacturing defects covered for 90 days from purchase
- Defective items: immediate exchange or full refund, no questions asked
- Damage from normal wear not covered after 90 days
- Damage from misuse or improper care not covered

ONLINE ORDER ISSUES
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
- Expedited shipping: 2-3 business days
- Express shipping: next business day if ordered by 2PM ET
- Lost package: investigation initiated after 7 days, reshipped or refunded
- Wrong item received: prepaid return label plus priority reshipping within 24 hours
- Missing item from order: refund or replacement processed within 24 hours

ESCALATION TRIGGERS
Escalate immediately to human agent when:
- Customer reports allergic reaction to product material
- Order value exceeds $500
- Customer reports identity theft or unauthorized account access
- Reward points dispute exceeds 1,000 points
- Customer has contacted support three or more times for same issue
- Customer explicitly requests human assistance

STYLEMAX STYLE ADVISORS
Personal styling available at select locations and online:
- Virtual styling session: free for Gold and Platinum members
- In-store styling appointment: free, available at flagship locations
- StyleMax app: AI-powered outfit recommendations based on purchase history
